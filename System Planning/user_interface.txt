A. Pages for All Users
= Home Page: Shows featured stores/products and navigation
= Login / Register Pages: Authentication for vendors and customers

B. Vendor Dashboard
= My Stores Page: List of stores with Edit and Delete buttons
= Create Store Page: Form to add a new store
= Store Detail Page: Shows store information and list of products
= Add/Edit Product Page: Form to add or modify a product
= Orders Page: Displays all orders related to vendor products

C. Customer Pages
= Browse Products Page: Displays all products (from all stores)
= Store Page: Displays products from one store
= Cart Page: Shows selected products, total price, and checkout button
= Checkout Page: Summary of order with confirmation
= Product Detail Page: Shows product info, price, and reviews
= Review Form: Form to add review for purchased product
